What You Get

More Than Just Numbers

Our trackers don't just collect data — they help you understand it and act on it. Here's what you unlock the moment you start training with Kinetic.

Instant Setup, Zero Hassle

Mount the sensor, pair with the app, and you're ready. No calibration, no complicated wiring — just plug and play.

Real-Time Feedback

See your metrics as they happen — correct your technique during practice, not hours later from reviewing footage.

Your Verified Performance Profile

Own your historical performance data. Send a verified biomechanical data profile to elite academies instead of subjective highlight tapes.

Injury Prevention

Track your cumulative physical stress and workload over a season. Know when you're at risk of overtraining before injuries happen.

Personalized Recovery & Nutrition

Based on your daily energy expenditure and workload, get AI-tailored caloric and hydration recommendations to optimize recovery.

Coach & Team Dashboards

Coaches get live multi-player dashboards with tactical heat-maps, team-wide analytics, and player comparison tools.
