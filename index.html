Race-Insight: Transform Raw Data into Actionable Insights.

Race-Insight is our revolutionary web-based time series analysis tool, designed to help you understand and leverage your performance data like never before.

  • Initial Focus: Sim racing telemetry from iRacing and Assetto Corsa.
  • Future: Scalable for comprehensive real-world motorsport data analysis.
  • Key Objectives: Efficient Data Handling (supports large telemetry files), Intuitive UI/UX, Seamless Telemetry Integration, Advanced Time Series Analysis (performance metrics, trends, predictions).
  • Cloud-based Platform: Access your data analysis, visualizations, and performance metrics from anywhere.

Moto-Insight: The Core of Your Performance Data.

Moto-Insight is our robust and fully customizable, CAN-based vehicle data acquisition system. Engineered for precision and reliability, it captures every crucial metric from your vehicle.

  • Key Features: CAN Bus integration, modular design, easy data acquisition from various sensors, user-friendly interface, Indian-developed, and highly customizable.
  • Unique Selling Points: Flexible data rates (up to 250 Hz), universal sensor compatibility, modular design for easy expansion, advanced data analytics, and a rugged, durable casing.
  • Specifications: Polling Rate (Upto 250 Hz), compatible with analog, digital, frequency, and CAN bus sensors, expandable storage, precise time synchronization, and includes a display, battery, CAN Bus, GPS, and IMU.
  • Core Components: Inertial Navigation System (INS), Display unit, Expandable storage, CAN Bus expansion modules, and CAN nodes.
