Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing

What does "perpetual license" mean?

A perpetual license means you own the software version you purchase forever. You'll receive free updates and bug fixes for 2 years. After that, major version upgrades (like v2.0) require a separate purchase.

What are AI tokens?

AI tokens are used to access AI-powered analysis features. Each analysis session consumes tokens. Tokens never expire, so you can use them whenever you need. Purchase additional tokens anytime.

Is cloud storage required?

No, cloud storage is completely optional. Race Insight works fully offline. Cloud storage is only needed if you want to sync your data across multiple devices or access it from anywhere.

Do you offer student discounts?

Yes! Student racing teams are eligible for a 50% discount on the software license. The discounted price is $24.50 (regularly $49). Contact us with proof of student/team status to receive your discount code.

Can I upgrade to a new version later?

Yes! When major versions are released (like v2.0), you can purchase the upgrade at a discounted price. Your v1.0 license remains valid forever, and you can continue using it even if you don't upgrade.

What payment methods do you accept?

When paid plans launch, we'll accept major credit cards, PayPal, and other secure payment methods. One-time purchases and subscriptions are both supported.
